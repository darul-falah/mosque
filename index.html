Friday Blessed Reading - Surah Al-Kahf

وَمَن قَرَأَ سُورَةَ الْكَهْفِ فِي يَوْمِ الْجُمُعَةِ أَضَاءَ لَهُ مِنَ النُّورِ مَا بَيْنَ الْجُمُعَتَيْنِ

"Whoever reads Surah Al-Kahf on Friday, it will illuminate him with light between the two Fridays."

- Hadith (Al-Hakim)

It is highly recommended to read Surah Al-Kahf every Friday. This blessed chapter brings spiritual light and protection throughout the week.
